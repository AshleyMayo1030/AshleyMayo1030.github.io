About Me

Hi, I’m Ashley Mayo, a graduate student pursuing a Master’s in Data Science with a background in finance. Through my academic journey, I’ve developed hands-on experience with machine learning models, data visualization, and big data technologies like Hive, Spark, and HBase—all through project-based learning.

This portfolio highlights the projects I completed during my master’s program, each designed to tackle real-world problems—from predicting Airbnb prices and analyzing pet adoption outcomes to exploring trends in childcare costs and fraud detection. I’m passionate about telling meaningful stories with data and translating complex problems into actionable insights.

Before entering the data science world, I worked in the financial services industry and currently hold Series 7, 63, and 66 licenses. My goal is to bridge that finance background with data analytics to grow into a well-rounded, impactful professional.
