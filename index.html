About Me

This portfolio highlights the projects I completed during my master’s program, each designed to solve real-world problems — from predicting Airbnb prices and analyzing pet adoption outcomes to investigating trends in childcare costs and detecting financial fraud. I’m passionate about transforming complex data into meaningful stories and actionable insights.

Before transitioning into data science, I built my career in the financial services industry, earning Series 7, 63, and 66 licenses. My goal is to combine a strong foundation in finance with data analytics to become a versatile and impactful professional.
